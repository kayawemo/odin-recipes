Enchilada Lasagna

Ingredents

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, choped
  • 1 1/4 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (28 ounce) can enchilada source
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice and cilantro

Steps

  1. Brown meat with onions and bell peppers, and season to taste. Add tomato paste and water. Let simmer.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten egg. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 370 degrees F (190 degrees C). Butter a 9 x 13 inch baking dish
  4. Begin layering all ingredients beginning with a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce. Follow with noodles, then
    ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until dish is filled.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) about 35 to 45 minutes, until bubbly. Let cool a couple of minutes
    before cutting.
